# Sirb
Sirb is a specialized web platform designed for students at the College of Computer and Information Sciences (CCIS) at King Saud University. It aims to help students build transparent academic profiles that showcase their skills, past projects, and areas of interest.

The platform addresses a common challenge among students: the difficulty of forming effective project teams due to limited visibility of peers’ skills  and previous collaboration experiences.

Sirb introduces a structured and trustworthy environment where students can:

-Create professional academic profiles
-Display their technical skills and project portfolios
-Evaluate peers through a controlled star-rating system
-Use predefined academic tags instead of open comments to ensure fairness and prevent misuse


The goal of Sirb is to support smarter team formation, enhance collaboration quality, and empower students to make informed decisions when working on academic projects
